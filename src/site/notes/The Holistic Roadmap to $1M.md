[[To reach $1M effectively\|To reach $1M effectively]]
The core of this strategy is moving away from trading time for money and toward building **Wealth Engines**. Here is the breakdown of how to reach that milestone effectively while maintaining your W2:

#### **1. The W2 "Backbone" Strategy**

Your job is your primary source of low-risk leverage. Use it to fund your other engines without compromising your wedding or ring savings.

- **Tax Efficiency:** Maximize the **Mega-Backdoor Roth** and **HSA**. This shields up to $40k+ annually from taxes, allowing your money to compound at 100% efficiency.
    
- **Credit Leverage:** Use your steady income to maintain the high credit score needed for your real estate and land play.
    

---
#### **2. Extracurricular "High-Octane" Income**

Leverage your Python and AWS skills to create high-margin cash flow that doesn't depend on your 9-to-5.

- **AI Automation Consulting:** Businesses are desperate for "Agentic Workflows." Charging **$150+/hr** for technical setup creates a massive cash bridge for your property goals.
    
- **Micro-SaaS/API Assets:** Build a tool once and sell it many times. Small tools for Alpaca or AWS users create **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** that adds value to your total net worth.
    

---
#### **3. The Algorithmic Alpha Engine**

Use your technical stack as a multiplier rather than a gamble.

- **Risk Management:** Prioritize strategies with a **2% max drawdown** to protect the capital you need for upcoming life events (wedding/house).
    
- **Compounding:** Aim for a consistent **20-25% annual return**. Reinvesting these gains is the fastest way to accelerate from $250k toward that million-dollar mark.
