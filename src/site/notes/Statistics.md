### Standard Deviation
- The "spread" of data at an index, or "slice" point
- How much stuff varies from the mean
> It is the average distance from the mean
	

### Mean
is of course the "average"

### Median
> **median** is the middle value of a dataset when ordered from smallest to largest. Unlike the mean, it isn't skewed by extreme outliers
